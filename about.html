About us

ANUJ has some of the finest infrastructure in the country for manufacturing a wide and exclusive range of Ceramics based Tile products. The infrastructure available includes built up space in excess of 5000m2 and equipment including the following: Fully automatic, State of the Art, German Block making plant with automated material handling systems Sophisticated Batching and Imported mixing plants with state of the art equipment for moisture control Varied Manufacturing lines including Vibropresses, Hermetic Presses, Wet Cast, Dry cast etc. Several processing and finishing lines for Finishing and Secondary processing like grinding, shot blasting, sealing etc. to concrete paving’s and tiles Complete In-house Testing Laboratory for Compressive, Transverse, Water Absorption and Abrasion Resistance Tests, Solid content, etc. Company owned fleet of transportation vehicles. We have a well-established infrastructure that includes all the advanced machineries and equipment’s required in the art of manufacturing. Our state-of-the-art plant for cutting and polishing improves the quality of products. Our processing units are equipped with various machineries like gang saw, dressing machine, edge polishing machine, polishing machines, and edge cutting machines to provide well-furnished products to our customers. For distribution network, we have a stock yard in Porur, a city of Chennai, which is quick emerging as an international market for tiles.
